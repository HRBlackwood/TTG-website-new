Who We Are

Welcome to the Guild

⚜

Townsville Tabletop Guild is the official tabletop gaming club of James Cook University. Whether you're rolling a natural 20, commanding Space Marines, or playing a land on turn one — you belong here.

We run sessions four days a week in a relaxed, inclusive environment where you can learn new games, find regular groups, and make friends who share your passion. No experience required — just bring your enthusiasm.

The Guild provides a shared library of board games, loaner rulebooks, dice and tokens, and a welcoming community that spans all skill levels. Open to all current JCU students and anyone 18 years or older.

Warhammer 40,000

The 41st Millennium awaits. We host matched play, narrative campaigns, and a dedicated painting station. Borrow starter armies to try the game before investing in your own force.

  • Dedicated painting nights
  • Narrative & matched play leagues
